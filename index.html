The week

Keeping it working

01

Coconut oil, measured

Kerala cooking leans on coconut oil — keep it to about 1 tsp per dish rather than a free pour. It's already counted into the curry estimates above.

02

Rice is flexible, protein isn't

If you're still hungry, add more fish/chicken/egg before adding more rice. Protein holds you full for longer per calorie.

03

Water before the meal

A glass of water or buttermilk 15 minutes before rice helps you naturally eat a smaller portion without trying.

04

Weigh weekly, not daily

Water weight swings with salt and rice intake day to day. Track a weekly average instead of reacting to one morning's number.
